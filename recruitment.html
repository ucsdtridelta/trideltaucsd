CURRENT OPEN BIDDING
Missed Formal Recruitment? Current Open Bidding (COB) is recruitment that happens outside of Fall Formal recruitment. It is a more informal recruitment process, which allows potential new members to get to know current members better.

Meet our sisters at events like beach bonfires, or get lunch at the Farmer's Market and froyo at Yogurtworld.

Our chapter hosts COB during Winter and/or Spring quarter.
WELCOMING OUR NEW MEMBERS
What happens after Bid Day? Accepting a bid from Tri Delta was one of the most exciting memories we've experienced at UCSD. We know that the recruitment process can be overwhelming, but after accepting your bid, our sisters immediately welcome you into the sisterhood. We've prepared many fun bonding events, like Big-Little Week, New Member Retreat, Beach Bonfires, and Movie Nights that you can look forward to after you join! New members receive Silver and Gold sisters and a Sponsor, who will all guide them through their new member process. These women will teach them Tri Delta's values and how to balance sorority obligations with their academic and social lives.
